Description of tracks separated by group:

BSGatlas


  • Genes: Results after merging various gene annotation resources. A click on individual gene shows all meta information related to it from the used resources.
  • Transcriptional Units The resources DBTBS, SubtiWiki, and BsubCyc provide transcriptional units. The collection of these are shown in this track. A click on an individual TU reveals which of these resources annotate it.
  • TSS/terminators/UTRs: We integrated the existing TSS and TTS data and associated these with the nearest genes in the respective direction of transcription. We annotate within the region inbetween with 5'/3' UTRs. We added based on the Trancriptional Units internal UTRs.
  • transcripts/isoforms: We inferred from the set of transcriptional units and TSS/TTS the full transcripts.
  • Operon: Transcripts that share at least one gene, are isoforms from the same operon. The here shown operons were computed according to that statement.

External Gene Resources


  • Unmerged Genes: Composite track that contains all resources that were used in the during gene merging. The track controls allows the selection of each resource and shows the priority level that were assigned to it.
  • Sensetive Rfam: A scan of the Bacillus genome at sensetive levels provided additional predictions for Rfam 14.1 families. We deemed as to sensetive, and were not explicitly included, however, we show them here.

External Transcription start/termination resources


The resources shown below provide sets of positions of transcription start and termination sites, as well as binding sites of transcriptional factors and untranslated regions (UTRs) as annotated in each resources. Each resource is represented by a composite track, which allows selection by annotation type.

Tiling-array signals (not shown by default)

This category shows the log2 raw signal of the tiling-array experiments conducted by Nicolas et al.. The data was retrieved from GEO (acession GSE27219).

Lifted probes

Information of the probes design for the tiling-array experiments. The intial design was for a a former genome assembly, such that the shown information is lifter over to the genome assembly used in this browser based on a pair-wise genome alignment.

CRISPR Targets

Predictions of gRNA targets for the entire genome with filtering for specificitiy, that is computed under consideration of potential off-targets. The specificity is computed with the CRISPRspec score. Per default, only guides with CRISPRspec larget 7 are shown. A click on a guide revels its a detailed page of its off-targets. Bot on-/off targets locationa also show information of potentially affected targets by listing overlapping annoatation in the BSGatlas. For the off-targets, mismatches up to 8 nt are considered (G-U wobbeling is not considered a mismatch in the off-target score computation). All predicted on-target sides also have the efficiency of the CRISPR estimated with the Azimuth score. Please note, that some few guides have potentially multiple on-targets with exact sequence matches. These are expliclty highlighted, and the alternative on-target sides are shown.
