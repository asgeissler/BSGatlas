BSGatlas v1 tracks


Description of tracks separated by group.

BSGatlas


  • Genes: Results after merging various gene annotation resources. A click on individual gene shows all meta information related to it from the used resources.
  • Transcriptional Units (TU): The resources DBTBS, SubtiWiki, and BsubCyc provide transcriptional units. The collection of these are shown in this track. A click on an individual TU reveals which of these resources annotate it.
  • Operon: TUs that share at least one gene steam from the same operon. The here shown operons were computed from the list of TUs by following this rule.

External Gene Resources


  • Unmerged Genes: Composite track that contains all resources that were used in the during gene merging. The track controls allows the selection of each resource and shows the priority level that were assigned to it.
  • Sensetive Rfam: A scan of the Bacillus genome at sensetive levels provided additional predictions for Rfam 14.1 families. We deemed as to sensetive, and were not explicitly included, however, we show them here.

External Transcription start/termination resources


The resources shown below provide sets of positions of transcription start and termination sites, as well as binding sites of transcriptional factors and untranslated regions (UTRs) as annotated in each resources. Each resource is represented by a composite track, which allows selection by annotation type.
